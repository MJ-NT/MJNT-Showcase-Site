About Me

Who am I? What do I do? How do I do it?

My name is Matt Taylor. I am a keen writer, narrative designer, and coder based in the UK with a background in languages and language teaching.

I created this website to showcase my website design and coding skills, as well as to give a platform and links to my other work, which includes writing, narrative design, and coding demonstrative of my skills in each area.

I got into coding via inkle's narrative scripting markup language ink.

I then progressed by studying Python, and completed Harvard's CS50x Introduction to Computer Science course. For the final project, I created a sophisticated 10k word interactive fiction browser game using Python, Flask, SQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and JavaScript.

You can find more about my work in the Writing section, and take a look at my art projects in the Art section.

If you would like to get in touch, please visit the Contact section.
